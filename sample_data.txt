=== Technology Companies ===

Apple Inc. was founded by Steve Jobs in 1976. The company is located in Cupertino, California.
Steve Jobs worked for Apple and created the iPhone. The iPhone is manufactured by Apple.

Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975. Microsoft is located in Redmond, Washington.
Bill Gates created Windows operating system. Windows is used by millions of computers worldwide.

Google was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998. Google is located in Mountain View, California.
Google acquired YouTube in 2006. YouTube is owned by Google and hosts video content.

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994. Amazon is located in Seattle, Washington.
Jeff Bezos is the founder of Amazon. Amazon provides cloud services through AWS.

=== Products and Services ===

iPhone is manufactured by Apple. iPhone is used by consumers worldwide.
MacBook is a laptop created by Apple. MacBook runs macOS operating system.

Windows is an operating system created by Microsoft. Windows runs on personal computers.
Office Suite is a productivity software created by Microsoft. Office Suite includes Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.

Android is an operating system created by Google. Android runs on mobile devices.
Chrome is a web browser developed by Google. Chrome is used by millions of internet users.

Alexa is a virtual assistant created by Amazon. Alexa is used in Echo devices.
Prime is a membership service provided by Amazon. Prime members get free shipping.

Apple and IBM have a business partnership. They work together on enterprise solutions.
